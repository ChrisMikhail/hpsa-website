Who we are.

Our Purpose.

Since the COVID-19 pandemic, there has been a dire need for informed conversations and education to be communicated between students and policy makers in the health sector. The HPSA acts a tool for this vital communication, fostering an understanding of global health, policies and future tech & innovation transforming the industry.

Our Philosophy.

Valuing ideas means valugint the future at HPSA, we ensure everyone's voice is heard, respected and valued. No discussion is too big or small

Our Mission.

At HPSA, we aim to provide a platform for students across the world to learn about the health policy field and connect with the experts across disciplines impacting global health. Whether through participating in crucial discussion in networking events, writing or research HPSA articles, every input further contributes to our mission.

Everyone is Welcome.

The HPSA encourages inclusivity in backgrounds, and expierences. We solemnly believe that valuing diverse work culture enable favorable outcomes and stronger universal comprehension.
